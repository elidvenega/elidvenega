About Me

I'm a self taught front end developer who loves technology and like's to challenge myself by learning new technologies. I have always had an interest in computers but did not know where to start until I saw a documentary about computers "Triumph of the Nerd" Bill Gates talk about computers and the impact they would have on people every day lives. I'm on a computer every day I thought to myself "might as well learn how computers work" so I went on youtube and look up tutorials and haven't stop since.
